Welcome to Guess The Movie!!!

This is my final project for the magnificent course CS50. It's a very simple game, I hope you like it.




RULES

The rules are quite simple.
Pick the number of players up to 4 and a timer difficulty for the answers, from 15 seconds to 60 (if you want to take it easy).

Each player gets 3 lives.

If you play in single-player mode, at the end the game will tell you
how many answers you got right within the chosen time.

In multiplayer mode, within one round, each player
gets to answer only once and then passes the keyboard to the next player.
If a player loses all their lives, they are out of the game.
At the end of the round, if there is only one player left with lives, they are the winner!
But if at the end of the round there aren't any players with lives left, then we have no winners.
