Welcome to Guess The Movie!!!

This is my final project for the magnificent course CS50. It's a very simple game, I hope you like it.




RULES

The rules are quite simple.
Pick how many players are playing and a timer difficulty for the answers, from 15 seconds to 60 (if you want to take it easy).

If you play in single-player mode, at the end of the 3 lives you have,
the game will tell you how many answers you got right at the timer difficulty you have chosen.

In multiplayer mode, for the whole turn, each player
gets to answer only once and then passes the keyboard to the next player.
If a player loses all their lives, it's out of the game.
At the end of the turn, if there is only one player left with lives, they are the winner!
But if at the end of the turn there aren't any players with lives left, then we have no winners.
